Department of CSE · Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore

I study the structure and representation of graphs — particularly word-representable graphs, semi-transitive orientations, and forbidden induced subgraph characterizations. My research sits at the intersection of combinatorics and theoretical computer science.

View Publications

Current Focus

Research Areas

Word-Representable Graphs

Characterizing graph classes via word representations, minimum-length word-representants, and extending hereditary graph properties to broader families.

Core Focus

Forbidden Induced Subgraphs

Developing minimal forbidden induced subgraph characterizations within specific classes — split, co-bipartite, and circle graphs — including open problems.

Combinatorics

Complexity & Algorithms

Weighted Clique and Independent Set problems in edge-distant hereditary graphs; NP-hardness reductions via semi-transitive orientation frameworks.
